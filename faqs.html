Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is the wedding?

Our wedding will take place on April 17, 2026. The ceremony will be held at First Baptist Church of Lincoln Gardens in Franklin Township, NJ, followed by the reception at The Marigold in Somerset, NJ.

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony begins at 10:00 AM. We kindly ask guests to arrive by 9:30 AM to allow time for seating.

What is the dress code?

The dress code is semi-formal. Traditional ethnic attire is also warmly welcomed.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Due to venue capacity, we kindly ask that you attend with only the guests listed on your invitation. Thank you for understanding.

Are children welcome?

Yes, children are welcome! Please make sure to include them when you RSVP so we can plan accordingly.

How do I RSVP?

Please visit the RSVP page on our website and enter your name exactly as it appears on your invitation. You’ll be able to submit or update your response there.

Can I update my RSVP after submitting?

Yes! You can return to the RSVP page and submit the form again at any time before the RSVP deadline if your plans change.

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please respond by March 15, 2026. This helps us finalize arrangements with our venues.

What if I’m unable to attend?

We completely understand. Please let us know by selecting “No” on the RSVP page. You’ll be missed!

Is parking available?

Yes, parking is available at both the ceremony and reception venues.

Will transportation be provided?

Transportation details, if applicable, will be shared closer to the wedding date.

Will the ceremony be unplugged?

We kindly request an unplugged ceremony. Please silence your phones and enjoy the moment with us.

Who can I contact if I have questions?

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us at shelvinancy@gmail.com.
